Welcome to Wagging Tails

We are located right beside our home in the idyllic countryside, and our brand new facility is designed with your four legged friend in mind! All of our kennels and indoor areas are climate controlled for maximum comfort. We have an indoor play area for bad weather days, but most importantly over 2 acres of lush lawn for exercise and all day outdoor play. Our outdoor area is secured with fencing to provide a safe and secure stay. Wagging Tails accommodates dogs of all sizes.

Daycare Fees

  • One Dog - $30.00
  • Two Dogs - $45.00
  • Three Dogs - $70.00
  • Four Dogs - $85.00
PLEASE NOTE

We only accept Drop Offs in the AM, and Pick Ups in the PM

Boarding Fees

  • One Dog - $55.00/Daily (INCLUDES Daycare & Overnight)
  • Two Dogs - $85.00/Daily (INCLUDES Daycare & Overnight)
  • Three Dogs - $135.00/Daily (INCLUDES Daycare & Overnight)

$50 deposits are required for all holidays listed on our website calendar. They must be paid within 24 hours and are non-refundable if reservation is cancelled within 72 hours of arrival.

Important Information

A temperament test IS REQUIRED and will be given at your first visit to our kennel. Failure of this test will result in the emergency contact being notified to pick up your dog, or your dog will be kenneled for the duration of their stay

Required Vaccinations

  • Bordetella
  • Rabies - all dogs 4 months of age must remain current with Rabies vaccine
  • Distemper
  • Parvo
** All dogs over 1 year old MUST be spayed/neutered **

What to Bring

  • All dogs must have a quick release collar. No pronged collars or chain collars allowed
  • Please portion food into individual meal bags and label with your dog's name
  • Bedding, blankets, food bowls (if your dog has their preference)
  • Any medication that is required. Please keep this in the original packaging
